BonnyBike was founded in 2017 by Gareth Williams and Saoirse McGregor, two fellow students of the School of Computing Science of the University of Glasgow who wanted to make a business out of their 2 passions: Cycling and Glasgow. Since Glasgow has long been an established vibrant cycling city where cycling is accessible, safe and attractive to all, creating a business that moves the mentality of the citizens towards the same direction that has made Glasgow see surge in people taking up cycling in the last decade, we believe we continue this growth well into the present and the future.

HOW TO USE OUR SERVICES

Hire a bike, ride it where you like, then return it to any docking station.

Easy to hire

Register and by joining, you will also be able to rent bikes in every BonnyBike dockplace around the city. Rent by entering the QR code on the bike or enter the bike number and your rental will begin.
